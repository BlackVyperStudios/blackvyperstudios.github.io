Company

BlackVyperStudios was founded on August 2nd, 2019 by Alexander Prosser and Damon Leven to create a name under which we can publish our projects and maybe find others, who want to work with us.
The most important thing for us is to use the best and most modern tools, to give our clients the best possible and efficient software. Our goal is to create games with unique and mind-breaking experiences.
Our games will mainly focus on story and gameplay.

Become a member

You want to be part of the BlackVyperStudios company, to work with us on awesome projects? If that's the case all you have to do is to contact one of our leaders or our official social media.
As one of our members, you can get access to our private cloud as well as private projects and libraries. And if you find any issues you can also fix them yourself.

Want to help us?

If you want to help us without becoming a member, you can do that by taking a look at our open-source projects on GitHub (you can see them on Projects > open-source).
If you found any issues, you can tell us by contacting
feedback@blackvyperstudios.software.
